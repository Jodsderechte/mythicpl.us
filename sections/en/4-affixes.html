What is an "affix"?

An affix is a modifier applied to a mythic keystone at set levels. Different combinations occur every week, with the combinations repeating on a set schedule.


Level 2 affixes
(these occur on all keystones, alternating weekly)

  • Fortified

    Non-bosses have more health and deal 30% more damage.

    Note: interrupting and dodging any/all enemy abilities becomes an absolute priority with this affix. Most of the time spent in the dungeon will be fighting beefy trash.

  • Tyrannical

    Bosses have 30% more health and deal 15% more damage.

    Note: Boss abilities are no longer trivial. Boss mechanics need to be respected (don't stand in stuff!). Some fights become endurance tests because they last so long.

Level 4 affixes
(one affix will occur at +4 per week)

  • Bolstering

    Non-boss enemies buff nearby allies on death, increasing maximum health by 15% and damage by 20%. This effect stacks.

    Note: it's important to kill all the trash in a pull as closely together as possible with this affix as enemies effectively heal as they gain max health. Enemies spawned by another enemy or boss ability do not trigger bolster. Bolstering falls off after 20 seconds.

  • Bursting

    Non-boss enemies explode on death, causing all players to suffer flat damage that scales with keystone level. This effect stacks.

    Note: This effect stacks, so it is safest to let the stacks drop before killing multiple enemies. Typicalyl only elite enemies trigger bursting. Now dispellable!

  • Raging

    Non-boss enemies at 30% health, dealing 75% increased damage until killed.

    Note: enemies with high damage targeted abilities or high damage AoE abilities become deadly with this affix. Be sure to focus fire enemies down so there aren't multiple enraged enemies at once. Soothe effects remove the enrage!

  • Sanguine

    Non-boss enemies will leave a pool of blood on the ground on death. This pool heals enemies and damages players for a percentage of their maximum health.

    Note: The pool's radius is 8 yds. Pools last 20 seconds.

  • Spiteful

    Spirits rise from the corpses of non-boss enemies and chase down random players, dealing HEAVY melee damage.

    Note: Whenever an enemy dies, it spawns a Spiteful Shade that will fixate on a random player. Their health and damage scales with keystone level. They lose 8% of their HP every second and can be crowd controlled.

Level 7 affixes
(one affix will occur at +7 per week)

  • Explosive

    Enemies have a chance to summon an Explosive Orb nearby that will explode after six seconds, inflicting damage 40% of the playerâ€™s maximum health as damage.

    Note: The explosive orb will spawn and begin casting a six second cast. If the cast goes off, the entire party will take 40% of their max hp in damage. They are AOE immune so you can't cleave them down! They can also spawn during boss fights. Can be line of sighted!

  • Grievous

    While below 90% health, players are afflicted with Grievous Wound.

    Note: Players bleed until they are healed above 90%. Does flat damage that scales with keystone level. Direct heals remove one stack of Grievous Wound.

  • Quaking

    Players will periodically Quake, interrupting spell casts of the player. Nearby allies take 40% of their maximum health as damage

    Note: spread out when you get a circle on you. If a player is casting when Quaking expires, they will be locked out of whatever school they were casting for 4 seconds!

  • Storming

    Tornadoes spawn around enemy creatures while in combat.

    Note: Grey swirlies appear on the floor indicating where a tornado will spawn. If a player is hit by the tornado, they take damage and are knocked back. The tornado disappears a player comes in contact with it.

  • Volcanic

    Enemies cause eruptions of flame near distant players. Additional volcanoes may appear near existing volcanoes.

    Note: Keep an eye out for the volcano animation under your feet or near you. Sometimes you may not have to move! Knocks you high in to the air in addition to doing damage scaled to the keystone level.

Seasonal affixes (+10)
(these occur on all keystones throughout the season at keystone level 10 and do not rotate weekly for the duration of the season)

  • Thundering (Season 1)

    Enemies have 5% more health. While in combat, players are periodically overcharged with primal power from Raszageth's unending storm. This power comes with great risk, and failure to discharge it quickly can have stunning consequences.

    More notes on this to come when the final affix is live. Two players get buffs, Mark of Wind and Mark of Lightning. While the buff is active, those players get a 30% damage and healing increase for 15 seconds. If the two marked players touch, the buffs are dispelled. If the buff is not dispelled by the end of its duration the buff becomes Primal Overload, stunning each player for 5 seconds and inflicting nature damage.
