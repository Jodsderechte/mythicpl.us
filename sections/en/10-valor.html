Completing mythic dungeons, callings, and keystone runs will now award you with Valor. Valor is a currency that allows you to upgrade gear earned via mythic dungeons.
If any player's Mythic+ rating goes up as a result of completing the run, the group is awarded an 65 extra Valor. This means a total of 200 Valor can be earned from a successful run.

The weekly Valor cap will start at 1500 and increase by 750 per week.[source]

Gear acquired from the end of run chest and Great Vault can be upgraded to a maximum item level of 415.

Gear slot Valor points per upgrade
Rings, cloak, wrists, necklace, off-hand 250

The cost to upgrade gear is static, meaning the cost does not change no matter the item level.
