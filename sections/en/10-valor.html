Upgrading Your Gear

Shadowflame Crests, Flightstones, and whatever else

I gotta rewrite all of this and I haven't read much about the new loot system. Gimme a day or two to get a more complete and (hopefully) simpler writeup done.
I do know at least these things:

  • This system is more complicated than Valor because why not take a perfectly fine system and just strip it out instead of iterating on it logically lmao
  • It's pretty simple though: do harder content, get better rewards
  • you can get Flightstones from endgame activities, including world content.
  • There is no limit to the amount of Flightstones you can GAIN a week, but you can only carry 2000 at a time.
  • Shadowflame Crests DO have a weekly cap (150 per type)
  • Shadowflame Crests only allow you to upgrade items to a certain item level. Shadowflame Crests and gear earned from harder end game activities have a higher upgrade floor and ceiling. [citation needed]
  • There is an account-wide 60% Flightstone discount that is applied when you receive an item that is a higher item level than you currently have.
  • In short, get an item to to an item level and it costs 60% less Flightstones to get other items to that item level.
